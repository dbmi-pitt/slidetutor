SlideTutor Laboratory

The Medical Tutor laboratory at the University of Pittsburgh Department of Biomedical Informatics is a unique cross-disciplinary team of researchers, students and programmers with expertise in education, cognition and intelligent systems. The laboratory is committed to:
  •  developing and deploying cutting-edge educational technology in medicine
  •  studying the cognitive basis of medical expertise and medical errors
  •  using medical informatics technology to enhance physician performance
As part of our educational mission at DBMI, we also work with graduate students and postdoctoral fellows on our currently funded projects and to develop new projects.
