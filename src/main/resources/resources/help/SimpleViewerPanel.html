Using the Virtual Microscope

SlideTutor incorporates a virtual microscope which has the same functionalities as a regular microscope, but is operated using a mouse.

Moving around the slide

To move around the slide, press and hold the left mouse button. Drag the mouse and then release the left mouse button. Repeat as needed until you are viewing what you want to view.

Zooming in and out

To zoom in:

Left click the button OR left click the mouse over the image.

To zoom out:

Left click the button OR right click the mouse over the image.


You can also zoom in and out using the scroll wheel on your mouse when your mouse is over the image.

Reset button

To return to the whole slide view, click the button.

Navigator window

The Navigator window provides a smaller view of the entire slide that you can use to see where you are on the slide and to control what is displayed in the main window. To open the Navigator Window, left click the button. The area of the slide you are viewing has a box around it. You can move to a different area of the slide using the Navigator Window by left clicking on a different area, or by dragging the box around by pressing and holding the left mouse button, and dragging your mouse. To close the Navigator Window, left click the button again.

Case Information button

To see the patient history and gross description for the case you are viewing, click the button. The case information will appear in a pop-up window. Click the OK button to close the pop-up window.
