SlideTutor is an educational system that can be used by pathology residents, fellows and practicing pathologists, to learn how to arrive at a correct pathologic diagnosis and how to accurately report their findings. SlideTutor uses “virtual slides” that you can examine just as you would look at cases under your microscope, but also provides space for you to describe what you are seeing and deciding, as well as space to type your report.

SlideTutor is an example of a new kind of computer based medical education – a cognitive tutor and simulator that provides immediate feedback as you work. SlideTutor monitors your work, and steps in with helpful explanations if you make a mistake. SlideTutor can also provide advice to help you move efficiently down the correct reasoning path. As you work, SlideTutor keeps track of how you are learning, and adapts its' interaction to fit your specific educational needs.

Studies in our laboratory have shown that SlideTutor is highly effective. On average, students are able to improve their diagnostic and reporting performance by a factor of four after as little as 4 hours of use of the system. [1][2]
